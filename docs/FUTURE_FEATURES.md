# Future Features

These features are important to AkaraConnect's marketplace vision but should remain honest placeholders during v1.0 beta unless fully implemented and tested.

## Delivery Rider Module

- Rider profile.
- Available orders.
- Navigation.
- Delivery history.
- Daily, weekly, and monthly earnings.
- Online/offline toggle.
- Ratings.

## Vendor Staff Management

- Manager, cashier, kitchen staff, and delivery assistant roles.
- Per-vendor permissions.
- Staff invitation and removal.
- Activity logs.

## CMS and Growth Tools

- Landing page sections.
- Hero section management.
- About, contact, FAQs, terms, privacy.
- Advertisements.
- Blog.
- Announcements.

## AI and Recommendations

Do not create fake AI outputs. Future AI should be clearly labelled and reviewed.

- Purchase-history recommendations.
- Location-based recommendations.
- Time-of-day recommendations.
- Popular foods.
- Trending vendors.
- Fraud and spam detection.
- Vendor insights.

## Subscriptions

Free plan:
- Limited menu items.
- Basic analytics.
- Standard listing.

Premium plan:
- Unlimited menu items.
- Featured listing.
- Advanced analytics.
- Marketing tools.
- Staff accounts.
- Priority support.
