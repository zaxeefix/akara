# Roadmap

## v1.0 Beta Stabilization

- Keep current Vercel frontend, Render backend, Neon database, and GitHub workflow stable.
- Improve customer registration, login, search, category browsing, and nearby discovery.
- Keep vendor onboarding approval-gated before a vendor can sell.
- Expand vendor dashboard cards for orders, revenue, reviews, verification, notifications, and business health.
- Keep Paystack as the primary beta payment provider while documenting Flutterwave, bank transfer, wallet, and cash-on-delivery architecture.
- Keep rider, vendor staff, support moderator, CMS, subscriptions, wallet settlement, and AI recommendations as honest beta placeholders until provider and operational workflows are ready.
