# Privacy and Ethics

## AI Bias

AI recommendations and fraud scoring may bias against new, rural, or low-volume vendors. Use human review for serious decisions and allow appeals.

## Fraud False Positives

Risk:

- Wrongly suspending vendors.
- Blocking legitimate customers.
- Flagging normal local behavior as suspicious.

Safeguards:

- Explainable signals.
- Human review.
- Audit logs.
- Appeal process.

## Loan Eligibility Fairness

Loan and insurance tools must not make automated final decisions. Financial partners and legal review are required.

## Location Privacy

- Collect only needed location data.
- Ask permission clearly.
- Provide manual location fallback.
- Do not expose precise customer location unnecessarily.

## Vendor Identity Privacy

- Protect verification documents.
- Show only public business data to customers.
- Restrict admin access to sensitive data.

## Payment Security

- Secret keys remain backend-only.
- Verify webhooks.
- Never trust client-only payment success.

## Chat Safety

- Provide report/block tools.
- Rate-limit spam.
- Keep support escalation path.

## File Upload Abuse

- Validate file type and size.
- Scan or moderate suspicious uploads.
- Keep verification documents private.

## Fake Reviews and Listings

- Link reviews to orders where possible.
- Moderate reported reviews.
- Require admin approval for vendors.

## Human Approval Requirements

Human approval is required before:

- Vendor suspension based on AI risk.
- Loan eligibility decision.
- Insurance eligibility decision.
- Permanent customer restriction.
- High-impact fraud action.
